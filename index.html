🇮🇳 Greener India Actions

Practical steps for rural and urban India

🌾 Rural Actions

🌱

Composting at Home

Turn kitchen waste into nutrient-rich compost. Use a pit or container, layer green and brown waste, and turn weekly.

💧

Rainwater Harvesting

Collect rainwater from rooftops. Simple systems can store 10,000+ litres annually, reducing groundwater dependency.

🏙️ Urban Actions

🚌

Public Transport

Using buses and metros reduces emissions by 70% compared to private vehicles. Plan routes and save money too!

🌳

Tree Plantation

Plant native species like Neem, Peepal, or Mango. Water regularly for first 2 years. One tree = 20kg CO₂ absorbed/year.
